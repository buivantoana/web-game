MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

For the best gaming experience, we strongly recommend installing the Albion Online desktop client on SSD.

Windows

  • Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • Intel/AMD CPU with SSE2
  • Graphics card with DirectX 10 or higher support
  • 4 GB RAM (8 recommended)

iOS

  • iOS 14.3 or later
  • 3 GB RAM (4 GB recommended)

iPhone:

  • Min: iPhone 7/8 Plus; iPhone 11 or newer recommended

iPad:

  • iPad 7th Generation, 10.2 or later
  • iPad Pro 10.5 (12.9”) or newer
  • iPad Air (2019) or newer
  • iPad Mini (2019) or newer

Android (Play Store)

  • Android 8.0 or higher
  • 64-bit ARM processor
  • Snapdragon 660, 820 or higher recommended
  • OpenGL ES 3.0 or higher support
  • 3 GB RAM (4 GB recommended)

Android (APK)

  • Android 6.0 or higher
  • 64-bit ARM processor
  • Snapdragon 660, 820 or higher recommended
  • OpenGL ES 3.0 or higher support
  • 3 GB RAM (4 GB recommended)
  • Do not install to an SD card. If possible, unmount the card before installation.

Macintosh OSX

  • Mac OS X 10.13.6 (High Sierra) or later
  • Intel/AMD CPU with SSE2
  • Metal compatible GPU with Apple support (Apple Silicon, Intel – HD4000 and newer (Ivy Bridge and newer), AMD – HD7000 and newer)
  • 4 GB RAM (8 recommended)

Linux

  • Ubuntu 20.04 LTS 64bit or SteamOS (other distributions may work, but are not officially supported)
  • Intel/AMD CPU with SSE2
  • SDL2: libsdl2-2.0-0
  • 4 GB RAM (8 recommended)
